Prepare Bill

Description
This use case generates the bill for checkout. It calculates the total amount to be paid.

Basic Flow
This use case starts when the actor is authenticated and proceeds to checkout.
1.The system adds all the items in the shopping cart.
2.The total amount to be paid is displayed.

Pre-Conditions
The actor must be logged in and authenticated.

Post-Conditions
If the use case was successful, the bill is generated and the actor is proceeded to checkout.
